Garlic Mashed Potatos

Ingredients:

  • 2.5 lb unpeeled quartered Potatoes
  • 2 tbs of Garlic
  • 1.5 sticks of Butter
  • 3/4 cup of Parmesean Cheese
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp of dried Oregano (1 tbs if fresh Oregano)
  1. Boil Potatoes for 45 minutes or until soft
  2. Strain potatos and return to pot
  3. Add remaining Ingredients to pot and mash potatos until smooth
  4. Enjoy!
